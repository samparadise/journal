Summer Pages
☀️ A summer writing adventure

Help your kid capture
a summer of memories.

Every few days a playful prompt arrives by text. Your kid sits down at a real keyboard, writes for a few minutes — silly or serious — and builds a summer-long collection of their own stories. No grades. No feed. No pressure.

Made for kids aged 11–99 · A phone to sign up, a keyboard to write

How it works

Tap any step for the details.

Every few days — not every day — a fresh question lands on their phone by text message: “Invent a new holiday,” “Describe a place you'd love to go.” That's why a mobile number is needed to sign up: the texts are how the whole thing works. Nothing to install.

Writing happens on a laptop or desktop — not the phone. It's a deliberate nudge away from thumb-tapping and toward sitting at a keyboard, thinking, and actually writing for a few minutes. A little room to breathe makes a big difference.

The phone is home base for looking back. Every finished prompt becomes a colorful tile, streaks build up, and they can reread everything they've made anytime — a growing scrapbook of the summer in their pocket.

All the nudges, check-ins and encouragement are handled automatically and tuned to each kid. On a roll? They'll get the occasional “nice work.” Gone quiet for a few prompts? It eases off, then gently invites them back when they're ready — no guilt trips. And no human is reading over their shoulder.

Safe by design

No public feed, no comments, no strangers — and no human reading their writing. Just your kid's own private pages.

No ads, no noise

Nothing to sell them and nothing to doom-scroll. A gentle text every few days, then done.

Better with friends

Invite their cousins or the whole friend group — everyone gets the same prompts.

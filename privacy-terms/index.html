Summer Pages (“Summer Pages,” “we,” “us”) is a summer writing app at july-journal.com. A writing prompt arrives by text every few days, and the writer answers it on the web. This page explains what we collect and the terms of using the service. Questions? Email hello@july-journal.com.

Privacy Policy

What we collect

  • Mobile number — used to sign you in and to text you prompts and reminders.
  • First name and birthday — to personalize messages and greetings.
  • Journal entries and any photos you add — the writing and images you create in the app.
  • Basic usage information — such as when you last wrote, to power streaks and reminders.

How we use it

  • To deliver your prompts and reminders by text message.
  • To save your entries and photos so you can read them back anytime.
  • To personalize encouragement and check-ins (see “Automated messages & AI” below).
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service.

Automated messages & AI

Prompts, reminders, and encouragement are sent automatically — there is no person reading everyone’s writing. To tailor these messages, your entries and activity may be processed by an automated AI service. We use this only to generate your personalized nudges and summaries, not to train public models.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your information, and we do not share it for anyone else’s marketing. We use a small number of service providers only to run Summer Pages:

  • SMS delivery (our text-messaging provider) — to send your texts.
  • Cloud storage — to store entries and photos.
  • AI provider — to generate personalized messages as described above.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of users.

Children’s privacy

Summer Pages is designed for young writers, generally with a parent’s or guardian’s involvement. If you are a parent and would like to review or delete your child’s information, contact us at hello@july-journal.com and we will take care of it.

Keeping and deleting your data

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. You can ask us to delete your entries and account at any time by emailing hello@july-journal.com.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no online service can be guaranteed perfectly secure.

SMS / Text Messaging

When you provide your mobile number at sign-up, you agree to receive recurring automated text messages from Summer Pages — writing prompts and gentle reminders.

  • Frequency: varies, typically about 2–4 messages per week.
  • Cost: message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt out anytime: reply STOP to cancel, or HELP for help.
  • Your number stays private: we never sell it or share it with third parties for marketing. It is used only to operate Summer Pages.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

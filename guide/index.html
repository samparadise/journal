Summer Pages

How it works ☀️

You're in! Here's the whole game in five quick beats.

📲

A prompt shows up by text

Every few days you'll get a text with a new prompt — a question or a fun idea to write about. No app to open, no schedule to remember. Just watch for the text!

💻

Write on a big screen

Open Summer Pages on a laptop or computer to write. Phones are perfect for reading your stuff — but writing happens at a keyboard, where you've got room to think.

✍️

A few minutes is plenty

Write as much or as little as you want. Add a photo, pick a mood, hit Post. It saves as you go, so you can't lose it.

🏆

Watch your collection grow

Every prompt you finish turns into a colorful tile on your phone. Keep a streak going, and look back at everything you've made anytime.

🌈

Miss a few? No biggie

Summer gets busy. If you skip some prompts, that's totally fine — jump back in whenever you feel like it. Your pages are always right here waiting.

What is Summer Pages? ☀️

Summer Pages is a friendly summer writing adventure. Every so often a new prompt arrives by text — a question, an idea, a little spark — and you write whatever it brings to mind.

No grades, no pressure. Just a few minutes to make something that's yours, and a growing collection to look back on.

How it works

  • 1You get a text in the morning with the day's prompt and a link.
  • 2Open it on your laptop and write — add photos, pick a mood, take your time. It saves as you go.
  • 3Hit Post when you're happy with it, and it joins your collection.
  • 4Keep a streak going and watch your trophy case fill up over the summer.

Who's it for?

Kids, families, and anyone who likes the idea of a low-key creative habit over the summer. It started as a project for two sisters and grew from there.
